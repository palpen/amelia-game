# I Know It! Game

A fun, interactive sight-word game designed for children ages 4-7. 

## Features
- Over 130 sight words (Pre-K to 2nd Grade).
- Drag and drop gameplay.
- Bright, kid-friendly interface.
- Keep track of known words and words to practice.
- View the lists of words by clicking the buckets.

## How to Play
1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
2. A random word will appear in the center of the screen.
3. Ask the child to read the word.
4. Drag the word to the "I Know It!" bucket (Star) or the "I Don't Know It" bucket (Thinking Face).
5. Watch the score increase!
6. Click on either bucket at any time to see the list of words inside.
7. Click "Start Over" to reset the game and shuffle the words again.
